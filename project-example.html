Neon Rush — a BloxdHop speedrun map

A neon-lit parkour course built for speed, with three branching shortcuts for players who know the map.

RoleMap design & scripting
ModeBloxdHop, 1–16 players
Timeline1 weekend, 2026
StatusLive in rotation

The idea

We wanted a BloxdHop map that rewarded players who came back to it — a course that's beatable on a first try but has real time to shave off once you know the shortcuts. Neon Rush started as a single tricky jump we kept testing on each other in a private world.

A good speedrun map teaches its shortcuts through level design, not a sign that says "shortcut here."

What we built

The main route is a straightforward hop-and-climb path anyone can finish. Around it, three optional branches shave off several seconds each but demand a harder jump or a blind leap of faith. Replace this paragraph with your own build narrative — what you tried, what changed, and why.

Design decisions

  • Checkpoints every 15–20 seconds, so a missed jump costs time, not the whole run.
  • Shortcuts are visible but not obvious — you have to actually try the risky jump to find them.
  • Kept the block palette to four colors so the neon accents stay readable at speed.
